Restructure of the Delaware Libraries Website

UX Designer

April 2023- June 2023

In my Introduction to Human Computer Interaction course we researched topics revolving around usability and heuristics. Our main project in this class was to redesign a website of our choice with a team chosen at random. We decided to redesign the Delaware Libraries Website. Each of us were assigned a specific page to redesign on our own. We then combined our pages and worked together for a final presentation.

To begin, I made a wireframe of the working website at the time.

The problems with the current website ___

Then, I created an improved low-fidelity prototype.

In my individual redesign I chose to mainly tackle the topics of site mapping, discoverability, and user signifiers.
